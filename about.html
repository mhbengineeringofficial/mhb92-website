About Us

Committed to Excellence in Engineering & Industrial Support

MHB92 Engineering & Supply

MHB92 Engineering & Supply (Pty) Ltd is a proudly 100% Black Female Owned enterprise established in 2022, based in Rustenburg, North West Province, South Africa.

We have positioned ourselves as a trusted partner in the engineering, construction, and industrial support sectors, delivering comprehensive solutions to mining, construction, and industrial clients across South Africa.

Our commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction has established us as a reliable service provider in the competitive industrial landscape.
